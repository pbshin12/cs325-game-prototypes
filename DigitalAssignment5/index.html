So my solution was to make this game a puzzle.

    The main controls for this game are just buttons where players can click on them to activate certain events. As part of this game, there will be a set of instructions and the player needs to carefully read over the instructions or else they will lose. When the player thinks that everything is safe, they will press the "Move Grandma" button to move the grandma to the goal post. If the grandma touches a laser, she will explode. Otherwise, if the grandma reaches the end, the player wins.

    THIS IS THE MANUAL THAT IS PART OF THE GAME:

    • If purple laser touches yellow laser, do not disable purple laser
    • The rightmost laser does nothing
    • If the yellow button turns green after 1 press, press it two more times to disable the yellow laser
    • The button controlling the red laser is the yellow button, but in red
    • Only on the second press of the yellow button can you safely disable the red laser
    • Do not touch the red button after disabling the red laser
